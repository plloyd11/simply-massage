Custom Designed Machines

Our core-competency is building Custom Automation Equipment. Generally, every machine we build is unique. Over our 50-year history we’ve served a wide-variety of industries and designed everything from semi-automated work cells to million-dollar, fully automated systems.

Built-To-Print Machines

Have a piece of equipment you want to have duplicated? Omnitech can use existing designs and prints to make repeat copies of equipment built by other manufacturers. Prints not available? No problem. Our engineer team can reverse engineer equipment and create drawings, electrical schematics, and programs.

Control Systems

Whether your application requires developing a new controls system complete with a Programmable Logic Controller (PLC), Human Machine Interface (HMI) and electrical enclosure, we have the experience and resources. We can also repair, reprogram and rewire older legacy systems. Need data exporting or remote access capability? No problem!
